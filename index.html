How we choose our picks

We started by identifying the most critically acclaimed and best-selling jogging strollers on the market. From there, we correlated expert reviews from trusted parenting and gear sites with long-term owner feedback from forums and retailer listings. We paid close attention to recurring themes around safety features (like wrist tether design and brake reliability), maneuverability at speed, and suspension quality. A jogging stroller is a long-term investment, so we prioritized models with a proven track record for durability and available replacement parts. We don’t claim to run timed trials in a lab. Instead, we rely on the consensus of thousands of miles logged by real users to identify what holds up and what rattles loose. This means we can honestly tell you where a lower price comes from (usually weight savings or fewer seat recline positions) and where extra spending truly pays off.
