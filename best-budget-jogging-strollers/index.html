It’s heavier and pricier, but dedicated runners will appreciate the confidence it inspires on descents and uneven paths. Our pick: Baby Jogger Summit X3.

Best for everyday value

The Chicco Activ3 is the all-rounder that checks almost every box at a moderate price. It runs well, folds easily, and transitions from jogging to strolling without fuss – making it the best pick for parents who want one stroller that does it all. Our pick: Chicco Activ3.

Best for light use and portability

When your jogging is occasional and you need something that’s easy to load into a small car or carry up stairs, the Joovy Zoom 360 Ultralight wins on weight and price. It’s less feature-rich, but for short, flat runs and walks, it gets the job done without weighing you down. Our pick: Joovy Zoom 360 Ultralight.

Our verdict

Whether you’re training for a 5K or just want a stroller that moves well on your daily walk, the budget jogging stroller category has you covered. The Chicco Activ3 remains our top recommendation for its balance of smooth ride, convenience, and affordability. If you’re a serious runner, consider the Baby Jogger Summit X3 for its hand brake and rugged build; if weight and cost are your primary concerns, the Joovy Zoom 360 Ultralight is a capable and very affordable alternative.
